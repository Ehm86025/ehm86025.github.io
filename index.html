Evan and Soccer

Evan Miller played soccer all four years of high school. He learned to love the game of soccer and began to grow fond of all of it's small details. His junior and senior year of high school he only lost a total of three game, including winning back to back region championships. During his senior year he was the team's starting right back and started almost all of the games except for a few where injuries forced him to not start. That year the team began the yesr ranked as the number seven team in Georgia, but in no time they were ranked number one in the state. The win that cemented their number one ranking was the eight to one drubbing of the team's biggest rival,
Johns Creek. The team went on an eighteen game win streak which eventually led them to be ranked number one in the nation for spring soccer by USA Today, and other soccer sites. The team was a number one seed heading into the state playoffs, but was still predicted by people to choke in the first round. The team would go onto the semi finals, but sadly would lose to the state champs to be. Overall Evan's experiance in soccer was one of the most memorable times in his life, and something that he would never forget. Check the Hooch Soccer Site
